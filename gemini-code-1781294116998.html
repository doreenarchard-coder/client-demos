Questions about this service

What are lab-grown diamonds?
Lab-grown diamonds are real diamonds with the same physical and optical properties as mined diamonds, typically at a more accessible price point.
Can you use my own stones or old gold?
Often, yes. Bring in your stones or heirloom pieces and we'll discuss how to incorporate or redesign them into a new custom piece.
How long does a custom ring take?
It depends on the design. After we agree on the details, we'll give you a realistic timeframe — it's best to start well ahead of any deadline.
Do I need an appointment for custom work?
Walk-ins are welcome, but for custom design, calling ahead at (954) 577-9000 lets us set aside time to plan with you.
