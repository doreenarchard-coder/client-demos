Precision Benchwork

Expert Metal Tailoring

Depending on whether your item needs to be expanded or condensed, we perform custom architectural bench sizing that protects the structural layout of your settings.

✨ Clean Sizing Down

To reduce a ring's circumference, we cleanly remove a precise micro-segment of the lower shank. The band is then skillfully fused using exact-match gold or platinum solder and finished to ensure the seam is fully invisible to the naked eye.

🛠️ Secure Sizing Up

To expand your piece safely, we do not simply stretch the metal, which can warp or thin the band. Instead, we cut the shank open and add an identical gold or platinum bridge insert, blending it perfectly to retain robust thickness.

The Mastery Process

Meticulous Ring Evaluations

We verify every structural dynamic under microscopy before altering any fine estate band or engagement piece.

01 Knuckle Metrics

We evaluate your hand dynamic at the counter to account for warm climates, knuckle structure, and stacking bands to identify the true targeted diameter.

02 Stone Thermal Protection

If your ring features fragile accent diamonds, emeralds, or sapphires, our master craftsmen carefully manage heat distribution to prevent structural stress on the settings.

03 Final Rounding & Polish

Every ring size alteration finishes with comprehensive precision rounding on steel mandrels, micro-polishing, and a deep sonic ultrasonic steam cleanse.

Technical Reference

Complex Sizing Quick Guide

Certain fine jewelry configurations require extra expertise. Here is a clear breakdown of how different band designs are managed on our master bench.

Band Styling Sizing Feasibility Bench Approach & Notes
Plain Gold / Platinum Bands Highly Feasible Standard cut and weld sizing. Can be quickly completed with perfectly hidden seam lines.
Solitaire Engagement Rings Highly Feasible Very straightforward, but requires protecting the center prong tension during cooling cycles.
Eternity Diamond Bands Complex / Restricted Because diamonds wrap fully around the circumference, sizing down requires removing stones, while sizing up requires adding a clear sizing bar.
Patterned / Engraved Details Moderate Complexity Sizing will gently break the repetitive pattern line. Our bench team re-carves detail lines by hand wherever possible.
