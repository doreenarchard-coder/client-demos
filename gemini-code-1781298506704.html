Precision Care

Our Core Restoration Services

No job is too small. We treat a simple chain solder with the exact same focus and technical rigor as a multi-carat platinum diamond re-mounting.

💍 Professional Ring Sizing

Expert adjustments for gold, platinum, and silver bands. Whether you need your engagement setting made larger or smaller, we ensure a seamless finish that maintains structural strength.

💎 Prong Re-tipping & Rebuilding

Worn-down prongs are the leading cause of loose or dropped diamonds. We build up fragile prongs and rebuild heavy-duty claws to lock your gemstones safely back into place.

✨ Stone Matching & Tightening

Missing a small accent stone or diamond slice? We carefully sort, match, and set replacement gems to perfectly mirror the color, clarity, and depth of your original piece.

📿 Chain Soldering & Clasp Fixes

We perform invisible, heavy-duty solders on fractured gold ropes, box chains, and tennis bracelets, alongside updating worn spring rings and heavy lobster clasps.
